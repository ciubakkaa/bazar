C — Gradient & Glass

Purple gradients, frosted glass cards, dreamy vibes. Trendy and Instagram-worthy but can feel generic — this is THE "AI-generated" look right now. Every student app looks like this.

My take: Option A already has real personality — it feels like something a student actually built, not a template. The warm tones + Space Grotesk + emoji stickers give it character without trying too hard. B is cool but maybe too serious for a student community app. C is what you specifically said you don't want (the generic AI look).
